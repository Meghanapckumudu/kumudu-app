Getting Started
How To Register App?

In Login Page - Select Sign Up Option And Fill All Mandatory Details in Registration Screen And Click on Register Button.

How to Join New Savings Group?
How to Join New Savings Group?

Select Enroll Plan From Menu Bar Or Join Plan Icon From Bottom Navigation Bar.

Select Any Suitable Saving Plans from Available Saving Groups List, Read Details Regarding New Joining Group in Details And Benefits Section.

Fill All Mandatory Details & Click on Join Button.

Now You have Successfully Registered to Savings Plan And Member Number Will Be Displayed on the Screen.

Next, Payment Screen Will Be Displayed. Here you can Pay by online Payment Mode, either By Cards / Net Banking / UPI.

How To Pay Installments Of Existing Savings Plan through the App?
How To Pay Installments Of Existing Savings Plan through the App?

All Joined Saving Plans Details Will be Listed under My Saving Plans Page. Select Your Plan. The Payment Screen will be Displayed. Specify / Verify Installment Amount. Select Payment Mode And Click on Pay Button.

After successful Online Payment, Transaction ID And Receipt Number Will be Displayed on Screen.

How To View All the Payment Details?
How To View All the Payment Details?

Select Your Savings Plan Under My Plans Page And In that Ledger Option you Can View All Paid Installment Details
